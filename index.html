Problem Statement

Context: More often than not students find themselves in a awkward situation that when they arrive at the gym or the library only to find it too populated for them to conduct their activities. Students and staff need a way to find out how populated a certain school facility is at a given time.

Problem: Rutgers is a very populated public school that facilities are often running at near maximum capacity. The current system only allows students to acquire the opening hours, but knowing how populated the Carr Library, or is there any open carrel on the second floor relies entirely on experience.

Proposed Solution: A web app that automatically acquire the userâ€™s location to learn how populated a place is and it should track the statistics for students to analyze.

App vs Website: This will not be a service students use constantly and it would not be a feature rich environment, so the students might not be willing to download the app for a feature that they might not use too often. Ideally this should be a web app or a web component that is embedded into the Rutgers recreation and dining website.
